What's Built
Full Feature List
💰
4-Ring Budget System
Debt Payoff, Savings, Wants & Fixed — each with its own color, progress bar, and status tracking.
❄️
Debt Snowball Tracker
Debts auto-sorted smallest to largest. Log payments, see projected payoff dates, celebrate wins.
🏠
Fixed Expenses System
Enter monthly bills once. Check them off as paid — they auto-post to the daily log instantly.
🛡️
Emergency Fund + Goals
$1,000 default emergency fund plus unlimited custom savings goals with projected completion dates.
🔥
Dual Streak System
Two streaks tracked simultaneously — consecutive days logged and consecutive days under budget.
📊
Weekly / Monthly / Quarterly Reports
Budget score, spending chart, category breakdown, wins & focus areas across all three periods.
💬
Two-Way Coach Messaging
Private conversation thread between each client and Gustavo & Greys. Pinned welcome note included.
🎯
Coach Triage Dashboard
Clients sorted by urgency — over budget first. Full detail view with notes, activity & PDF export.
📄
PDF Report Generator
Branded G&G reports printable to PDF. Weekly, monthly, or quarterly with personal coach notes.
✨
Premium Black & Gold Design
Gold sparkles, shimmer buttons, Alex Brush script, dark mode throughout — built to match your brand.
